Last Updated: June 2026

ChattQuisitions is a brand operated by Mental Pictures LLC ("we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit chattquisitions.com or communicate with us, including via SMS text messaging.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our website or submit a form:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Investment criteria (property types, budget, preferred areas)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about properties or investment opportunities

By submitting your phone number through our forms or otherwise providing it to us, you consent to receive SMS text messages from ChattQuisitions / Mental Pictures LLC related to real estate investment opportunities, buyer/seller inquiries, and related business communications.

  • Message frequency may vary.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP at any time to opt out of future messages.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of any purchase or service.

Carrier Disclaimer: Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

We do not sell or share your mobile phone number or opt-in information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business (CRM, hosting, communication platforms)
  • Investment partners or buyers, only when relevant to a specific deal you've expressed interest in

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and maintain business records, or until you request deletion.

Your Rights
